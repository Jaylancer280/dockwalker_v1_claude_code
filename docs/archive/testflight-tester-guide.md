> How to install and test DockWalker on your iPhone.

## Step 1: Install TestFlight

TestFlight is Apple's app for testing apps before they're on the App Store.

1. Open the **App Store** on your iPhone
2. Search for **TestFlight**
3. Download and install it (it's free, made by Apple)

## Step 2: Join the DockWalker Test

You'll receive either a **link** or an **email invite**.

**If you received a link:**

1. Tap the link on your iPhone
2. It opens TestFlight
3. Tap **Accept** → then **Install**

**If you received an email:**

1. Open the email from TestFlight/Apple on your iPhone
2. Tap **View in TestFlight**
3. Tap **Accept** → then **Install**

**If you received a redeem code:**

1. Open the **TestFlight** app
2. Tap **Redeem** (top right corner)
3. Paste the code
4. Tap **Install**

## Step 3: Open DockWalker

1. DockWalker appears on your home screen like any app
2. Open it
3. **Sign up** with your email and a password (8+ characters)
4. Check your email for a **confirmation link** — you must click it before you can log in
5. Log in and complete onboarding

## Step 4: Test These Flows

Please test as much as you can. Here's what matters most:

**As Crew:**

- [ ] Sign up and complete onboarding (pick Crew)
- [ ] Set your availability (dates + location)
- [ ] Browse daywork jobs on Discover
- [ ] Apply to a job (swipe right or tap Apply)
- [ ] Switch to Permanent tab and browse permanent jobs
- [ ] View your profile — does it look right?
- [ ] Add a vessel experience to your profile
- [ ] Upload an avatar photo
- [ ] Try Docky (AI advisor) — ask about STCW requirements

**As Employer (switch hats):**

- [ ] Go to Profile → tap the hat switcher (⇄ Employer)
- [ ] Post a daywork job
- [ ] Post a permanent job
- [ ] Add a vessel
- [ ] Review applicants (once crew have applied)

## Reporting Issues

When you find something broken or confusing:

1. **Screenshot it** (press Side button + Volume Up)
2. Tell us:
   - What you were trying to do
   - What happened instead
   - What device you're on (e.g., iPhone 14, iOS 17)
3. Send it to [FOUNDER CONTACT METHOD — WhatsApp/email]

## Updates

When we push a new version, TestFlight will notify you. Just open TestFlight and tap **Update**. You don't need to reinstall.

## FAQ

**Q: Do I need a special account?**
No — just sign up with any email address.

**Q: Is this the real app?**
It's a test version. Your data is on a test server. When we launch for real, you'll sign up again on the production app.

**Q: Will it cost anything?**
No. Testing is free.

**Q: How long does the test last?**
TestFlight builds expire after 90 days. We'll push updates before then.

**Q: I can't log in after signing up.**
Check your email for the confirmation link. You must click it before logging in.

**Q: The app shows a white screen / error.**
Force-close the app (swipe up from app switcher) and reopen. If it persists, screenshot it and send it to us.
